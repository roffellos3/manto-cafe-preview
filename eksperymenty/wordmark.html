Night-run read (numbers, not vibes)

Anton is the closest condensed-black analogue to the packaging wordmark (cap proportions and blackness in the same family as HN-CondensedBlack); Archivo Black is heavier but noticeably wider — it changes the lockup's rhythm; Oswald at 700 is condensed but reads a full weight-class lighter. Recommendation: Anton as the cross-OS wordmark face IF the client's real brand font is unavailable. Real answer remains the packaging designer's font files.
